ABOUT PEARL IVY AND THE FOUNDER EDWARDS SHARON

Pearl Ivy events was founded in the 2000 in London by Edwards-Umukoro Sharon. Our unforgettable and beloved founder, Sharon, began her journey as a luxury tour operator and her passion for planning, meticulous details and organization led Pearl Ivy to become one of the leaders for high-end and exclusive events in London and all over England.

The goal of Pearl Ivy is to leave your guests satified with the beauty of you and your event put together.

Our team is composed of professionals who come from different industries and expriences,and together they represent the perfect team: from the luxury travel services to the elegant wedding.

Teamwork is the key to our success!

Pearl ivy will ensure an outstanding Event and fulfill even the most challenging requests by creating workable solutions for a smooth and uninterruputed day. The team creates exclusive events that exceeds the expectations of every individual guests and will make sure every detail is accounted for with a concierge-style approach.

With a friendly and trustful team environment,many international Companies and important personalities have relied on Pearl Ivy to put on exclusive luxury events in the hearts of the audience.And with London as its beautiful backdrop,anything is possible. Pearl Ivy invites you to create your unforgettable exprience!

Our important network of relationships and relational partnerships with local,national and international suppliers and leaders, will make our events so special.
